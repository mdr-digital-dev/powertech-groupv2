Our Mission

PowerTech Group of Chicago is a Licensed Security Company and UL Listed provider headquartered in Arlington Heights, Illinois. For over two decades, we have designed, installed, and maintained safety, security, and IT infrastructure for commercial and industrial clients across the Chicago metropolitan area.

We believe that every business deserves access to enterprise-grade technology — and a partner who takes the time to understand your unique operations before recommending a single piece of equipment. Our approach is consultative, our installations are precise, and our service is built to last.

From single-door access control to multi-building security systems, from a simple network switch to a fully managed IT infrastructure — we handle it all, under one roof, with the same team you'll know by name.
