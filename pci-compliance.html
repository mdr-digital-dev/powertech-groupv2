PCI Compliance

Structured, straightforward compliance — so you're ready when the auditor arrives.

Home/ IT Services/ PCI Compliance

If your business accepts credit cards, PCI DSS compliance is mandatory. PowerTech handles the technical implementation — network segmentation, firewall configuration, documentation, and testing — so you can focus on understanding your obligations, not building infrastructure.

What PCI Requires

The 12 Requirements, Handled

PCI DSS covers network security, access control, data encryption, vulnerability management, and ongoing monitoring. Meeting each requirement demands properly configured firewalls, network segmentation to isolate your cardholder data environment, documented policies, user training, and regular testing.

PowerTech configures active firewalls, enforces router port restrictions, and builds dedicated VLANs for POS systems, data networks, and voice — keeping payment data cleanly isolated from the rest of your infrastructure.

When Your Auditor Shows Up

You'll Be Ready

PowerTech produces the complete network diagrams and documentation that assessors need to verify your environment. We establish limited access controls with full audit logging, set up remote access security protocols, and manage user authentication. For businesses completing a self-assessment questionnaire (SAQ), we guide you through every requirement with plain-language explanations.

Annual compliance reviews keep your posture current as your business and the PCI DSS standard evolve. We've guided dozens of Chicago-area businesses through compliance — from small retailers to larger organizations undergoing formal QSA audits.
