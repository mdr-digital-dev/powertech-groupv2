Burglar Alarm Systems

Our commercial burglar alarm systems are engineered for reliability in demanding environments. We design, install, and program systems that integrate motion detectors, door/window contacts, glass-break sensors, and duress capabilities — all connected to UL Listed central station monitoring.

  • UL Listed central station monitoring 24/7
  • Instant mobile alerts & remote arm/disarm
  • Multi-zone, multi-partition configurations
  • Integration with cameras & access control
  • Battery backup & redundant communication paths
